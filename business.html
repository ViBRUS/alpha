Foundation Story

``Entrepreneurship is living a few years of your life in a way that most people won’t, so that you can live a life that most of them can’t``

The company – Felis Leo Ventures was started with a motive to promote sustainability and renewable energies. During a casual discussion on the terrace late at night, the co-founder twins discovered their love for entrepreneurship and the art of doing business in the city of Bhubaneswar and beyond. They always had in mind that one must aim for the moon. Even if you don’t get the moon, at least you’ll hit the stars.

The Co-Founders, kept brain storming on what to start with and eventually came up with a plan to start with an IT company which would provide software, web development and app development services to customers in Bhubaneswar. Slowly getting through the twist and turns at teenage years, the duo also began a marketing and entertainment wing which would serve as a full scape marketing consultant, PR and events management team for their clients. Eventually, after entering the college lifestyle and understanding the need of the hour, the twins couldn’t keep away from venturing into the genre od sustainability and renewable energies. That is where the entire story of innovations began. The technologies were thought upon, innovated and launched in the market. The response for the products was amazing and very motivating for the duo to keep innovating further.

This urge for innovation got them to work on bio-organic batteries as a substitute to lithium powered ones since the metal and chemical would ultimately turn out to be a waste for the society and would become toxic in the later years. The research just began with a sentence picked up from a bio-chemistry book of 1964, which said, “Peptides could have potential benefits in electrolytes.” There has been no turning back since then. The duo also plans to venture into the airline service industry in the future. They are also working on the 5th vertical of the company, i.e. the Leisure and dine across India and beyond.

Felis Leo Ventures shall also have a Brew Pub and few other food joints under its portfolio soon. The work is already in progress. As founders the girls have 5 years of experience in business with different verticals like IT, Marketing and PR, Sustainability etc.

An undying love for machines, cars and aircrafts got the girls to create Felis Leo Ventures in 2015 and the journey has been a pure roller coaster ride since then. The twin founders, through their company – Felis Leo Ventures are doing their best to reduce carbon footprints and make the world a better and happier place to live in.
